Challenge Timeline

Key dates and milestones for the XLPSR Challenge at IEEE ICIP 2026

An Official Grand Challenge at IEEE ICIP 2026

Tampere, Finland • 13–17 September 2026

Important Registration Note

All participants must complete the official registration form before accessing the dataset and submission platform. The timeline below outlines the challenge phases and key deadlines.

Feb 11, 2026 Current Phase

Official Challenge Launch

Challenge documentation, rules, and registration form go live. Participants can begin the registration process to access the dataset and submission platform.

~ Feb 20, 2026 Dataset Release

Development Set Release

The Development Set with full ground truth labels becomes available to approved registrants. This set includes complete low-resolution video frames and license plate annotations for local model development and validation.

March 01, 2026 Public Leaderboard

Public Validation Set Release

The Public Validation Set (without ground truth) is released and shared with registred participants. Participants can submit their super-resolution outputs for automated evaluation and public leaderboard ranking. This set is used for benchmarking against other participants.

May 13, 2026 Final Deadline

Final Submission Deadline

Deadline for submitting final solutions and the mandatory two-page extended abstract describing the approach. All submissions must include both super-resolution outputs and the extended abstract through the CodaBench platform.

May 14 – May 31, 2026

Internal Evaluation Period

The organizing team evaluates all submissions on the blind test set using the automated scoring protocol (Character Error Rate with custom scoring rules) followed by expert panel assessment for final winner selection.

June 10, 2026

Extended Abstract Acceptance Notification

Authors are notified about the acceptance of their two-page extended abstracts for inclusion in the ICIP 2026 Grand Challenge proceedings. Feedback may be provided for camera-ready version preparation.

July 1, 2026

Camera-ready Abstract Submission

Deadline for submitting the final camera-ready version of the two-page extended abstract, incorporating any requested changes from reviewers and following IEEE ICIP 2026 formatting guidelines.

13–17 September 2026 Conference

IEEE ICIP 2026 Conference & Winner Announcement

Grand Challenge results and winners are announced during a special session at IEEE ICIP 2026 in Tampere, Finland. Winning teams will be recognized, invited to present their approaches, and receive awards.
